WHAT IS IGNITE?

IGNITE is the annual flagship technical festival of SVKM’s NMIMS Navi Mumbai, engineered (pun intended!) by the students of The School of Technology Management and Engineering.

Last year, we experienced the first-ever completely virtual technical festival, making it the first of its kind. The online nature of events didn’t impede our spirits and we hosted an array of exciting events over a span of 4 days, receiving fervours participation from over 30+ colleges across the country.
